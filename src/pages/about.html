Our Project

ShowMeCoders is a cooperative effort undertaken by Missourians participating in the Grow With Google Scholarship Challenge sponsored by Google and offered through Udacity.

This project was created as part of this three month long program of instruction to demonstrate our grasp of the lessons learned during the coursework and to gain the valuable skill of working as a team.
